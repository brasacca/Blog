Entry 1: Importance of Semantic HTML (9/2/2021)

The concept of semantic HTML is an incredibly important practice in web development that should not be overlooked. An example used in an article I read referenced the <p> tag "This tag is semantic and presentational because people know what paragraphs are and browsers know how to display them" 1. Alternativly, tags like <b> and <i> are not, because they only definte the appearance of text rather than the context. This distinction is especially important when it comes to search engine optimization, as well as accessability so that screen readers know how to better interpret the text of a webpage. Also, more generally, sematics make your code cleaner and much easier to maintain and read by yourself and other developers looking at your code.
